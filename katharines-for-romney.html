Number of Katharine's born in 1992, by states that voted Romney in 2012 Presidential election

This map accounts for two variables: the voting outcome of red states in the 2012 presidential election, and the number of Katharine's born in that particular red state in 1992 (that is, the number of Katharine's born in that state that would have just reached the voting age of 21 years old in the year 2012).

The idea behind this map is to find any sort of correlation between the number of Katharine's born in that state in 1992 (now 21 years old) and the voting percentage in that state for Romney in 2012. The darker the state is, the more Katharine's were born there in 1992. All states pictured voted Romney.
